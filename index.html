Gluten Free

Most of our delicious fudge is gluten free, but some flavours have biscuits and other tasty delights added. If you have any allergies or dietary requirements, we’ll be happy to help you find the ones that suit you best.

Handcrafted with Love

Our delicious fudge is made daily in our little shop by our expert fudgetarians. If you have any flavour favourites you’d like to see in shop, like our page and leave us a comment on facebook.com/fudgeittaupo.

Fudge Flavours Galore

We take our fudge seriously, but we like to have a little fun with it. Our fudgetarians love thinking up new and exciting flavours to bring to your bellies – we have over 50 flavours and more coming. Keep your eye out for new flavours weekly!

The Fudgetarian’s Workshop

It all started when two couples whose jobs in tourism were affected by COVID, thought Fudge It, let’s start the tastiest shop in Taupo, a fudge shop. On the 25th of September Fudge It was officially opened inside YOU Travel on 40 HueHue Street. Come on in and let our fudge loving fudgetarians help you find your perfect fudge.

We have fudge for every occasion:

  • Corporate Gift Ideas
  • Birthday Party Favours
  • Holidays and Special Occasions
  • Dessert Platters
  • Gift Boxes
